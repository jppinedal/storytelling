2. 🗣️ Her voice rises to unite her community, explaining the coming changes and the need to protect their home.

3. 🏗️ The arrival of a corporation with its machines and hard hats. Ishwa confronts them, firm and wary of the promise of "progress".

4. 😠 The excavators advance on sacred land, a visual clash between industry and ancestral heritage, reflected in worried faces.

5. 💨 The windmills impose themselves on the landscape, a symbol of clean energy for some and a scar on the land for others.

6. 💪 Concern turns into determination. Ishwa knows she must act, not just for herself, but for the future of her community.

7. ✊ "There is no development without respect." The message is clear and direct. The community unites in a peaceful protest, demanding to be heard.

8. 🏛️ Ishwa's struggle takes her to bigger stages, where her voice, dressed in tradition and pride, resonates strongly.

9. ✨ The story ends with a look of hope. The new Wayúu generation, with big, dream-filled eyes, represents the future.
